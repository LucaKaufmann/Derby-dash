# Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-02-05

Derby Dash is a local-first Hot Wheels tournament management app. This policy explains what data the app accesses, how it is used, and your choices.

## Summary

- Derby Dash stores tournament data locally on your device.
- Derby Dash does not require an account and does not collect or transmit personal data to our servers.
- Photos you choose or take are stored on your device and are only used inside the app.

## Data We Collect

Derby Dash does not collect analytics, tracking identifiers, or personal information. We do not operate a backend service for the app.

The app may access the following data on your device:

- Camera access: to take photos of cars.
- Photo library access: to select photos for cars.
- Photo library add access: to save car photos to your library if you choose to do so.

All tournament data, settings, and photos are stored locally on your device.

## How We Use Data

- Car photos are used to display and identify cars inside the app.
- Tournament data is used to run brackets, standings, and history.

We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to third parties.

## Data Storage and Security

- Data is stored locally on your device using a local database.
- If you uninstall the app, local data is removed by the operating system.

## Children’s Privacy

Derby Dash is designed for family and kid-friendly use. The app does not collect personal data from anyone, including children.

## Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, we will change the “Last updated” date above.
